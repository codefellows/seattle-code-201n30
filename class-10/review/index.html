About Us

Launched in 2022, Salmon Cookies combines two of Pat's passions; his love for sweet and salty confections, and his commitment towards fish conservation and maintaining healthy ecosystems. His cookies are suitable for both humans and salmons to enjoy, and come in an assortment of mouth-watering flavors.

Pat's savory confections pair well with a light and crisp green or jasmine tea. Flavors include Original Sea Salt Salmon, Blackened Salmon, Smoked Salmon, Spicy Salmon & Seaweed, Three-Flavor Salmon & Basil, and Ultimate Salmon Heaven.

His sweeter confections go perfectly with a cold glass of milk, an early morning coffee, or hot cappuccino. Flavors include Brown Sugar & Honey Salmon, Lemon Zest Salmon, and Mild Sweet Salmon.

There is a perfect flavor for every member of your family to enjoy!
